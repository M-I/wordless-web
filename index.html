WORD LESS WEB

  1. 1. Click and drag above button to your browser’s bookmarks bar.
  2. 2. Go to any website: Facebook, Youtube, Twitter, Huffington Post...
  3. 3. Click the button.
  4. 4. Enjoy.

Word Less Web is an experiment that allows us to look at web sites in a completely different way. A simple browser plug-in serves as a filter that make the text on any site invisible by turning the text and background colors into white. Only thing you see are the pictures against a blank background.

Removing the text also means removing the context for the pictures. Now you can see the pictures purely for what they are without names, labels, definitions, and purposes. It completely changes the way we perceive images on sites like Facebook, Youtube, Twitter, and Huffington Post. Our interaction with friends, culture, social connections and news instantly becomes more mysterious and open-ended for each person’s imagination.

The whole web also seems a little calmer and quieter when words disappear.
